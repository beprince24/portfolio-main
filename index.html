How I Work

My process in four steps.

A structured, repeatable approach to every technical challenge — from first contact to long-term resolution.

01

Discover

Understand the environment, existing systems, pain points, and business goals through structured intake and assessment.

02

Diagnose

Systematic root-cause analysis using logs, monitoring tools, and direct testing — never guessing, always verifying.

03

Resolve

Implement the fix with minimal disruption. Test thoroughly, validate with stakeholders, and confirm service restoration.

04

Document

Create a clear record of the issue, resolution, and preventive measures. Update runbooks and share knowledge.
